Artist Opportunity - The Alfred Arcade Banner Commission 2022
Monday 10th January 9:00am – Monday 14th February 2022 9:00am

‘a space’ arts are looking to commission a new artwork that will be printed on a banner at the back of The Alfred Arcade on Old Northam Road, overlooking the railway. The work could be a collection of words or a phrase containing a message to daily commuters or visitors to the city; or it might refer to artistic activity.

This is the third in a series of banner commissions on Old Northam Road. Please see Appendix 2 for examples of previous works, Your Existence is Everything by Jo Carson and Move Past This by Isaac Fravashi.
